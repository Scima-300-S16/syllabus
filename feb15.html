Assignment 2:
A2a :: Non-Digital Communications Selfie Part A
Document your analog communications for a 24 hour time period. How do you communicate to drivers and bikers when crossing the street on foot, when ordering coffee in a busy shop, or late at night in a rough neighborhood. Consider vocal and non vocal communications. You may use some categories/ metadata that you used for your digital observations, but you will also need to add new ones. Note the difficulty in establishing metadata categories for non-digital communications. Organize your categories in a spreadsheet, TSV, or CSV. Label file A2a_lastname_firstname.filetype and submit to the A2 folder within SCIMA-300-S16 in Google Drive.

A2b :: Non-Digital Communications Selfie Part B
Consider your A2a non-digital communications data. You may work in any visual environment you wish to visualize your non-digital communications. How do you understand context, meaning and shared understanding in these communications? Be creative and think outside of the box in regards to your visualizations. Do however, make the elements of your visual (size, color, position) directly correspond to your data set. Make 2 sketches. Sketches should each have a title and any necessary text to explain the data being visualized. Labels for graphs may be necessary. A viewer should be able to look at the visual and understand what the data implies.
Bring to class Feb 15.
